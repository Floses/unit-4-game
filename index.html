GEM MASTER

Welcome to GEM MASTER! At the beginning of the game you will be given a random number at the top of the play area to your right.

Below are four gems. When a gem is clicked it will add a number value to your total score seen below in gold.

The number value of the gem is hidden until clicked.

You win the game when your total score is equal to the randomly given number.

Win or lose, each new game comes with completely new random numbers for all gems and target.
